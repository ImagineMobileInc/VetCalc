Treatment Recommendations

1. Decontamination: Induction of emesis is recommended if ingestion occurred within the last 2 hours.

2. Activated Charcoal: 1-2 g/kg orally.

3. Antidote: Vitamin K1 at 3-5 mg/kg/day divided BID-QID for 30 days.

4. Monitoring: PT/aPTT at 48-72 hours post-ingestion and 48-72 hours after discontinuation of Vitamin K1.
